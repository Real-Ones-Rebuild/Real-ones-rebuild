About Marquis Owens

Real Ones Rebuild was born from my story — one of pain, prison, perseverance, and purpose.

My name is Marquis Owens. I’m a father, a certified peer specialist, and a survivor of addiction and incarceration. But most of all, I’m someone who decided to break the cycle and rebuild a better future — for myself, my son, and my community.

I created Real Ones Rebuild as a platform for healing, truth, fatherhood, and redemption. It’s a reminder that your past does not define your future — and no matter how far you fall, you can always rise again.

We rebuild together.
