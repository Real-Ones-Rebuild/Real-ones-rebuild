Real Ones Rebuild Podcast Topics

Drugs & Rehabilitation

We'll speak on real-life addiction — the moments that broke us, and the paths we took to heal. This includes detoxing, relapse, treatment centers, and how recovery is more than just staying sober — it's about transforming the way we think and feel.

Time in Prison

We’ll talk about what it’s like being locked up: the mental toll, the survival tactics, the people you meet, and the moments that wake you up. We’ll share the good, the bad, and the clarity that sometimes only comes from hitting rock bottom.

The Child in Me Speaking

This is the heart of it — the inner voice that never got to speak. These episodes explore how childhood trauma shaped us, what we wish we could say to our younger selves, and how parenting now gives us a chance to break cycles and rebuild with love.
