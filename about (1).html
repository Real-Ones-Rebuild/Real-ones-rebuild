About Marquis Owens

Real Ones Rebuild isn’t just a brand — it’s a movement born from pain, resilience, and purpose.

My name is Marquis Owens. I'm a Certified Peer Specialist, a mandated reporter, a trained de-escalation professional, and a graduate with a degree in Psychology. But my journey started long before any of that.

I grew up in a single-parent home, faced the struggles of fatherlessness, and carried childhood trauma I never had words for. I found comfort in football and classrooms where I was often the only Black student in advanced classes. But as life got harder, I turned to drugs — first pills, then heroin and cocaine — chasing numbness instead of healing.

After years of battling addiction, I was incarcerated in 2017. And while prison took away my freedom, it gave me something more valuable: clarity. I got clean. I confronted my demons. And I found my purpose — to be the father I never had, and to be a light for others still fighting through their darkness.

What Real Ones Rebuild Means

It means we own our stories. We admit our past without letting it define our future. We fight for healing. For fatherhood. For faith. And we do it together.

This isn’t just about my comeback — it’s about helping others build theirs. Through speaking, writing, peer support, and this platform, I’m here to show what rebuilding really looks like from the inside out.

HoldenStrong — named after my son — is more than a name. It’s a promise to him, and to every parent trying to break the cycle. We don’t quit. We don’t fold. We rebuild together.
