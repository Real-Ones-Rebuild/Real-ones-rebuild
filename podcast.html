Real Ones Rebuild Podcast

Conversations that heal. Perspectives that challenge. Stories that rebuild.

🎙 What to Expect

This podcast is a space for real talk — about life, pain, growth, and redemption. We'll dive into the kind of conversations that usually stay behind closed doors. Every episode is rooted in truth and transformation, offering listeners a raw, unfiltered look into what it means to rebuild your life after addiction, incarceration, and trauma.

📚 Future Episodes in the Works

  • “How It Started” – My first experience with drugs and the mindset behind that first “yes.”
  • “Prison Made Me Sober, But God Made Me Clean” – The difference between detox and true recovery.
  • “Fatherhood Ain’t Optional” – What it really means to be a present father when the odds are against you.
  • “My Dad Wasn’t There — Here’s What That Did to Me” – Emotional insight from the child’s perspective.
  • “Letters to the System” – An open conversation about the flaws, hope, and pain in family court.
  • “When Real Ones Relapse” – What no one talks about in recovery, and how to keep rebuilding after setbacks.

🤝 Guests & Voices to Come

We’re bringing in real people — those who’ve lived it. Expect to hear from:

  • Parents who grew up without a mom or dad and how that shaped their adult choices
  • People in recovery from substance use who are now mentoring others
  • Formerly incarcerated fathers and mothers who are rebuilding their families
  • Children of addicts sharing their side of the story
  • Social workers, court advocates, and peer specialists with lived experience

🎧 Why This Podcast Matters

Our stories are often silenced or told for us. But here, we own our narrative. This podcast is a mirror for some, a megaphone for others, and a safe place to learn, cry, and grow. Whether you’ve been through it or love someone who has — these episodes are made for you.
