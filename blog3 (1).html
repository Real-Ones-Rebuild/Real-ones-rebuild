Fighting for My Son in Family Court

Being a father is the greatest title I’ve ever held. But for me, being a father hasn’t been easy — it’s been a war. Not because I didn’t want to be present, but because the system didn’t want me there.

I’ve stood in courtrooms with no lawyer, no handouts — just truth and heart. I’ve had to fight for time with my son like it’s a privilege, not a right.

Why I Never Gave Up

Because my son deserves better than silence. Because I know what fatherlessness feels like. Because I’m fighting for legacy, for presence, for healing.
